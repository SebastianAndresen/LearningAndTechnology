Tutorial for teachers on how to effectively conduct online teaching.

The objective for this tutorial is to develop an easy-to-understand tutorial for teachers on how to effectively conduct online teaching with a step-by-step guide on what to have in mind when teaching online and designing e-learning solutions. Throughout the tutorial, the teachers will test their knowledge on small tasks developed using h5p.org - a html based tool that allows interactive online teaching.

Created by Gustav B., Jakob T. and Sebastian A. October 2020.
